n99 - Talent Promoting Social Network

N99 100th Episode
Click image to zoom

N99 = Network99 = THE ERA OF THE CREATIVE, RISING UP! Artists have always yearned for a platform for them to simply publish, without fees, to the world. Network99's Social Network is built with this in mind, and gives 99% of all revenue generated straight back to the content's creator, and keeps only 1% for continued development of the system.

History

Envisioned and being developed by a team led by visionary Tim Coomber, Network99 is the technology that will allow everyone on earth to share their artistic talents with the world, and receive either 99% or 100% of the profits made from the GET requests on SAFE. The current corporate state of the internet was a complete let down for Tim and other artists & musicians around his time, as he explains in this episode of SAFE Crossroads. Now--armed with SAFE--he is determined to flip the industry inside-out.

Features

A platform for artists of all types to share their music, videos, poetry, scripts, sculpture models, drawings, and any other type of creative talent with the world. All views (GET requests) equal payment to the creators, so if they create popular content then their digital wallets will fill up very quickly, with no big corporation like iTunes or Google Music or any of that rubbish getting in the way (or taking a cut). It will be the perfect platform for everyone to share, enjoy, and earn livings from their creative talents, allowing them to live their work and explore their crafts infinitely deeper.

Launch Date

It was expected that the network would launch at the end of last year, (and this app soon after,) however an amazing new coding language came out called "Rust" that turned out to be perfect for the SAFE Network to be created with. So, the team decided to restart the whole network from scratch in Rust. This means we will have to wait a few more months to see the network or this app. Follow it on Github!
